Priest of Stars

In a galaxy where faith fuels power, doubt can be deadly. Arun the Darkblade is a Smart-mouthed, low-ranked Exorcist barely scraping by on the fringes of the galactic order. His prayers barely flicker, his followers are few, and his reputation is nonexistent. In a universe where Exorcists broadcast their divine battles to millions, Arun is nothing more than background noise. Until the Pope—the supreme spiritual leader of the galaxy—is found dead. In his final act, the Pope names twenty-two successors to inherit the Throne of Light. Arun is one of them. Now thrust into a brutal war of faith against the galaxy's most powerful Exorcists, Arun must fight to survive in a deadly competition where betrayal is gospel and power is everything.

About Author

Maxime Harold is a storyteller with a passion for crafting epic adventures that blend high-octane action, intricate world-building, and characters you can’t help but root for. When Maxime isn’t busy conjuring new dimensions or pitting heroes against impossible odds, you can find them lost in their favorite sci-fi and fantasy novels, perfecting martial arts moves they’ll never actually need, or hunting down the perfect cup of coffee to fuel their creative bursts. Inspired by everything from legendary folklore to modern-day pop culture phenomena, Maxime Harold writes stories that remind readers of the power of courage, teamwork, and standing up for what’s right—even when the odds are stacked against you. With a knack for weaving explosive battles, heartfelt camaraderie, and a touch of the unexpected, Maxime Harold invites readers to step into their worlds, where adventure is endless, danger is imminent, and the only limit is your imagination. Brace yourself—because once you open the first page, there’s no turning back.
